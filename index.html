Goal of the game

The first player that get's all of his/her's four pawns into the pawns home base wins the game.

Getting on the start line

To get on the board you must throw a six. When all four pawns are in their starting position you may throw three times until you throw a six


Crossing the board

Dangers lukrs on the board as other players can kick you off the board and you have to start all over again


Getting inside home base

To get inside home base you can't throw higher than the spots avaliable. The more pawns are inside home base the harder it gets to get the next one in as the pawns can't stand on top of each other


The winner

The first player that gets all four pawns inside home base wins the game
